Frequently Asked Questions

No account is needed to use Offline Mode. You can create and manage local tournaments right away without signing in. An account (email/password or Google Sign-In) is only required to access Online Mode features like groups, real-time tournaments, and group chat.

Offline Mode stores all tournament data locally on your device using SQLite — no internet required. Online Mode uses Firebase to sync data in real time across all participants, supports group chat, push notifications, and lets players from anywhere join your tournament. Both modes support full round-robin tournament management and player statistics.

For Offline Mode: tap the "+" button on the home screen, add your players, and the app automatically generates a round-robin schedule. For Online Mode: first create or join a group, then create a tournament inside the group. Other group members can see the tournament and matches update in real time.

Once you create a group in Online Mode, you can share the group link or invite code with your friends. They need to sign in to the app and join using the code. Group admins can also search for users by their profile name and invite them directly.

Yes, PES Arena is free to download and use. The app is supported by ads. All core features — offline tournaments, online groups, match management, player stats, and group chat — are available for free.

For offline data: uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. For your online account: go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account in the app. This will permanently remove your account and associated data from our Firebase servers. You can also email us at taipv.swe@gmail.com to request account deletion.
